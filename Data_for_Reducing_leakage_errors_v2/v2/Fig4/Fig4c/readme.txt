Explanation for csv-data files: 
gate_error_list: 1d array, error per gate from all individual RB experiments conducted during 24 hours after gate calibration
incoherent_error_list: 1d array, incoherent error per gate from all individual purity RB experiments conducted during 24 hours after gate calibration
leakage_error_list: 1d array, leakage per gate from all individual leakage RB experiments conducted during 24 hours after gate calibration

Here, we used a gate duration of 6.67 ns (incl. 0.41 ns padding) and FAST DRAG-L pulse.
